About Me

Creating a safe space for healing and growth

Hello, I'm Sarah Hudson, a qualified and experienced counsellor based in Manchester. I understand that taking the first step towards therapy can feel overwhelming, and I'm here to provide a warm, non-judgmental space where you can feel heard and supported.

With a background in person-centred therapy and extensive experience working with individuals and couples, I specialise in helping people navigate life's challenges, including relationship difficulties, grief and loss, anxiety, depression, and personal development.

My approach is collaborative and tailored to your unique needs. Together, we'll work towards understanding your experiences, developing coping strategies, and building resilience for a more fulfilling life.
